# Subscribe

Get updates from us by email. We send action alerts, event invites, and the occasional thank-you. We don't sell or share your address.

## Sign up

[**→ Subscribe to our newsletter**](http://localhost:9001/subscription/form){ .md-button .md-button--primary }

(Replace this link with your real Listmonk subscription form URL once you've created a list. The default form lives at `/subscription/form` on whatever domain you're running Listmonk under.)

## What we send

- **Action alerts** — short, time-sensitive asks. ~2 per month.
- **Newsletter** — longer pieces, campaign updates, member spotlights. Monthly.
- **Event invites** — anything we're hosting or co-hosting in your area.

## Unsubscribing

Every email we send has a one-click unsubscribe link at the bottom. We honour it immediately. No retention emails, no "are you sure?" dark patterns.

## Privacy

We use [Listmonk](https://listmonk.app), a free and open-source mailing list manager that we run on our own server. Your address is stored on our infrastructure, in this country, and goes nowhere else.
